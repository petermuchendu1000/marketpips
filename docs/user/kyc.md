# Identity verification (KYC)

> Why MarketPips verifies identity, what you need, and how the process works.

## Why verify?

KYC ("Know Your Customer") verification helps keep MarketPips safe and compliant
with East-African financial regulations. It protects your account, prevents
fraud and money laundering, and unlocks higher withdrawal limits.

## When you'll be asked

- Before your **first withdrawal**, or once your activity/withdrawals cross
  regulatory thresholds.
- If we need to re-confirm your details.

You can browse, deposit, and place bets before completing KYC; verification is
primarily gated on cashing out.

## What you'll need

- A government-issued **ID** (national ID, passport, or driver's licence).
- Your **personal details** matching that ID.
- Sometimes a **selfie** or proof of address, depending on your country and level.

## The steps

1. Go to **KYC** (or you'll be prompted at withdrawal).
2. Enter your details and **upload clear photos** of your document. Make sure the
   whole document is visible, in focus, and not cropped.
3. Submit. Your status becomes **pending review**.
4. Our team (or automated checks) reviews it. You'll be notified when your status
   changes to **verified** or if we need anything else.

## Your data & privacy

Your documents are stored securely and used only for verification and compliance.
See the [Privacy policy](../legal/privacy.md) for how we handle, retain, and let
you request deletion of your data.

## Troubleshooting

- **Rejected or need re-submission?** You'll get a reason — usually a blurry or
  cropped image. Re-upload a clearer photo.
- **Taking long?** Reviews are usually quick; during busy periods they can take
  longer. You can keep trading while you wait.
