# Portfolio & profit/loss

> Understand your positions, balances, and how profit and loss (P&L) is
> calculated in real time.

## Your balances

- **Available balance** — money you can bet with or withdraw right now.
- **In positions** — money currently staked in open markets. Not withdrawable
  until those positions close or the markets resolve.

## Open positions

Each position shows:
- the market and your side (**YES**/**NO**),
- **shares** you hold and your **average entry price**,
- **current value** — your shares valued at the market's *current* price,
- **unrealized P&L** — current value minus what you invested. This moves live as
  prices change (it is *unrealized* until you exit or the market resolves).

> P&L direction is shown with an **icon and a sign**, not colour alone, so it's
> clear whether you're up or down regardless of how you see colour.

## Realized vs unrealized P&L

- **Unrealized P&L** — paper gains/losses on positions you still hold.
- **Realized P&L** — locked-in results from positions that have resolved or been
  closed. This is the money actually added to (or removed from) your balance.

Your **total P&L** is the sum of both, and your profile tracks lifetime stats
(volume traded, number of bets, win rate).

## When markets resolve

When a market you hold resolves:
- **Winning** shares are paid out to your available balance automatically.
- **Losing** shares expire worthless.
- The result moves from unrealized to **realized** P&L, and your win-rate updates.

## Transaction history

The Portfolio page lists your recent transactions — deposits, withdrawals, bets,
payouts, and any adjustments — each with a timestamp (in your local East-Africa
timezone) and a reference you can quote to support.

## Currency display

All amounts are shown in your **preferred currency**. You can change your
currency and interface language from your profile / the footer language switcher;
values are converted using current exchange rates.
