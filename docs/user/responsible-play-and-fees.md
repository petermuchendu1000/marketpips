# Responsible play & fees

> Play within your means, understand the costs, and know where to get help.

## Responsible play

Prediction markets involve **real money and real risk**. You can lose what you
stake. A few principles:

- **Only stake what you can afford to lose.** Never bet money you need for
  essentials.
- **It's not a way to make guaranteed income.** Prices reflect uncertainty; even
  likely outcomes sometimes don't happen.
- **Set your own limits** and take breaks. Chasing losses usually makes them
  worse.
- **18+ only.** MarketPips is for adults of legal age in your country.

### Getting help

If betting stops being fun or feels hard to control, please seek support. You can
contact us via the **Help** page to request cooling-off or account limits. If you
or someone you know needs help with gambling, reach out to a local support
organisation.

## Fees

MarketPips keeps fees transparent — you always see them **before you confirm**.

- **Platform fee.** A small percentage may apply to trades and/or winnings
  (configured per the platform fee rate). The exact amount is shown on the bet
  and payout screens.
- **Payment/provider fees.** Mobile money providers may charge their own fees on
  deposits or withdrawals. Any such fees are displayed at transaction time.
- **Currency conversion.** If you transact in a currency other than your account
  currency, conversion uses current exchange rates, shown before you confirm.

There are **no hidden charges** — if a cost applies, it appears on the
confirmation screen.

## Fairness

Market prices are set by the automated LMSR market maker based on real trading —
not by MarketPips picking winners. See
[How betting works](./how-betting-works.md). Markets resolve to the **real,
verified outcome**; disputes are handled through the platform's moderation and
resolution process.
