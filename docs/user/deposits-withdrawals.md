# Deposits & withdrawals (mobile money)

> How to add money to MarketPips and cash out, using the mobile money wallets
> available in your country.

## Making a deposit

1. Go to **Deposit**.
2. Pick your provider and enter the **amount** and the **phone number** linked to
   your wallet.
3. Approve the prompt on your phone (e.g. the M-Pesa STK push / enter your PIN).
4. Your MarketPips balance updates automatically once the provider confirms —
   usually within seconds. If it's pending, give it a moment; confirmations
   arrive asynchronously.

Deposits are credited in your account currency. If you fund in a different
currency, MarketPips converts using the current exchange rate.

## Withdrawing

1. Go to **Withdraw**.
2. Enter the amount and the wallet/phone to receive the funds.
3. Confirm. Withdrawals are sent to your mobile money wallet (a "B2C"
   disbursement).

Requirements & notes:
- **KYC may be required** before withdrawing, especially above certain amounts.
  → [Identity verification](./kyc.md)
- **Limits** apply per transaction and per period for your safety and to meet
  regulations. You'll see the applicable limits on the withdrawal screen.
- You can only withdraw your **available balance** — money currently staked in
  open positions is not withdrawable until those positions are closed or the
  markets resolve.

## Fees & exchange rates

- Any platform or provider fees are shown **before you confirm** a transaction.
- Currency conversion uses live rates refreshed regularly. See
  [Responsible play & fees](./responsible-play-and-fees.md).

## Troubleshooting

- **Deposit not showing?** Confirm the provider prompt was approved; pending
  deposits settle once the provider callback arrives. Check **Portfolio →
  transactions**.
- **Withdrawal failed?** Ensure your KYC is complete and the receiving number is
  correct and registered. Failed disbursements are automatically reversed to your
  MarketPips balance.
- Still stuck? Use the in-app **Help** page to contact support with your
  transaction reference.
