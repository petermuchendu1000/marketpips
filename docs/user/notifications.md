# Notifications

> Stay informed about your bets, markets, and money — on the channels you choose.

## What you'll be notified about

- **Bet confirmations** and payouts when markets resolve.
- **Deposit / withdrawal** status updates.
- **Market events** you care about — closing soon, resolved, or cancelled.
- **Account & security** notices, and important announcements.

## Channels

MarketPips can reach you through:
- **In-app** notifications (the bell / Notifications page) — always on.
- **Email** — to your registered address.
- **SMS** — to your registered phone (great for low-data connections).
- **Push** — if you've installed MarketPips as an app and allowed notifications.

## Managing your preferences

Go to **Notifications → Preferences** (or your profile) to turn **email**,
**SMS**, and **push** on or off independently. A channel only sends when you have
it enabled *and* we have a valid destination (e.g. a verified email or phone).

Turning a channel off stops those messages immediately; in-app notifications
remain available so you never miss anything important.

## Tips

- Keep **at least one channel on** for money-related alerts (deposits,
  withdrawals, payouts).
- Add MarketPips to your home screen to enable **push** and get instant updates
  even offline-friendly.
