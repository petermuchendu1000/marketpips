# Responsible Play

> **Launch template — pending legal review.** Mirrored in-app at
> `/legal/responsible-play`. User-facing version: `docs/user/responsible-play-and-fees.md`.

_Last updated: July 2026_

## Principles
- **18+ only** (or legal age in your country).
- **Only stake what you can afford to lose.**
- **No guaranteed income** — prices reflect uncertainty.
- **Don't chase losses**; take breaks.

## Player-protection tools
Users can request deposit/stake limits, cooling-off, or self-exclusion via
[Help](/help). Support requests are actioned by staff and recorded.

## Identifying harm
Warning signs: betting more than planned, borrowing to bet, hiding activity,
chasing losses, anxiety. We surface responsible-play messaging and signpost help.

## Getting help
We signpost local problem-gambling support organisations and provide account
limits on request.
